Article 7 of the Price Law

Effectiveness: Currently in force Level: National Law
open_in_new

Operators shall truthfully and accurately mark the prices of commodities and the charging standards for services in accordance with the provisions of the competent pricing department of the State Council, and shall specify the names, origins, specifications, grades, pricing units, prices or charging items, charging standards and other related content of commodities.

Operators shall not use false or misleading price representations to deceive consumers or other operators into trading with them.
